# Code of Conduct

SpatialScope Agent follows a simple course-project conduct rule: be respectful,
scientifically cautious, and specific.

Expected behavior:

- Discuss bugs and scientific limitations with evidence.
- Keep feedback actionable and kind.
- Do not post secrets, private datasets, or identifying information in issues.
- Treat biological interpretations as provisional unless independently
  validated.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, publishing secrets,
or presenting unsupported biological claims as confirmed conclusions.
